How the body shapes the way we think - a new view on intelligence

  • Rolf Pfeifer, Josh C. Bongard
  • Published 2006
  • Psychology, Computer Science
    • How could the body influence our thinking when it seems obvious that the brain controls the body? In How the Body Shapes the Way We Think, Rolf Pfeifer and Josh Bongard demonstrate that thought is not independent of the body but is tightly constrained, and at the same time enabled, by it. They argue that the kinds of thoughts we are capable of have their foundation in our embodiment -- in our morphology and the material properties of our bodies.
